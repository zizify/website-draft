Hey, there! I'm Andy.

As a full stack developer, I work mainly with Node.js and Express on the back-end, and React on the front-end. I also have experience working with both SQL and non-SQL databases, with emphasis on developing REST APIs. I don't limit myself professionally to working with these techs, however, and I'm very open to learning and working with new languages and frameworks.

Apart from web development, my other passion is linguistics—particularly contact phenomena and nonconcatenative morphology. Some day, I'd love to marry the two and work on language acquisition software.
